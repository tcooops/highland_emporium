Jura 16oz Heavyweight

Highland Emporium offers a comprehensive range of 150 Jura Tartan Designs.

This 16oz pure wool Jura fabric is the perfect weight for a traditional kilt, with the benefit of a stain resistant finish as standard to give added durability.

*The fabric colour & design shown on this website should only be used as a guide. A swatch of current stock is available upon request.

Strome 16oz Heavyweight

We supply a heavyweight range of fabric consisting of over 500 authentic tartans (500-515gm per linear metre 138 cm wide/16oz per linear yard 54" wide).

It is considered by many to be the best kilt making fabric in Scotland and is the tartan of choice for many kiltmakers, regiments and pipe bands worldwide.

*The fabric colour & design shown on this website should only be used as a guide. A swatch of current stock is available upon request.
